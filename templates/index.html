What do we do at Circuitrader?

We provide a way for tech enthusiasts to meet and save the planet together by reducing e-waste

Why do we do it?

E-waste is a huge problem in the world today. We want to help reduce the amount of e-waste that is produced by providing a way for people to request or offer their electronic components to others.
